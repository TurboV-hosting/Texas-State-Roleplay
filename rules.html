Server Rules & Regulations

Compliance is mandatory for all citizens within Texas State Roleplay.

General Rules

  • I. Age Requirement: This is a 16+ server. Anyone found to be underage will be removed immediately until such time as said person(s) is of age.
  • II. Respect: Be respectful and have some common sense. We have a ZERO TOLERANCE policy towards discriminatory language, hate speech, slurs, etc. Toxic behaviors are not welcome. This includes Transphobia and Homophobia.
  • III. NSFW Content: Gore and other NSFW content is not allowed in public channels. However, what you do in private is your own business.
  • IV. Advertising: Please refrain from self-promoting or advertising for another gaming community. Streamers have the role for a reason and poaching city members is not tolerated.
  • V. Exploiting: Abusing any system within our discord or in-game mechanics/groups are strictly prohibited.
  • VI. Hardware: A Working / Clear microphone is REQUIRED to be in Texas State Roleplay. Excessive feedback from your mic may result in being temporarily removed from the city so the issue may be fixed.

Reports & Bans

Issues involving other players or staff may be submitted through the ticket system in the Texas State RP discord.

  • If you need help immediately in-game, use the /report command.
  • If you need general help, submit a general ticket stating the issue in the #create-a-ticket channel found under Assistance.
  • If you are reporting another player, please submit a compliance ticket.

Punishment Information

Punishments listed below are strictly a guideline for staff members. Different situations will call for different punishment types and lengths.

  • Class A: 1 day ban & 5 warning points
  • Class B: 3 day ban & 10 warning points
  • Class C: 7 day ban & 15 warning points
  • Class D: 30 day ban & 20 warning points
  • Class E: Permanent ban

* Warning points will be totaled up after each punishment. A total of 100 warning points will earn you a permanent ban. Warning points become void after 6 months.

In-Game Information

I. Character Names

Character names need to be somewhat realistic. None of this "Ben Dover" or "Anita Bathe" nonsense.

II. Maintain your Roleplay!

  • Breaking character at any point in time will result in an immediate Class A ban. Play out the scenario and report later.
  • Compensation for lost items requires proof of ownership and loss if rules were broken.
  • Calling other players out on breaking rules while in-city will result in a ban for all parties. Let staff handle it.

III. /OOC Usage

/ooc is strictly for reporting actively occurring in-game issues. It is NOT a space for chatting. Take it to discord.

IV. Server Restarts / Storms

  • Roleplay should continue as normal before and after a restart.
  • MANDATORY RETURN: If you are in a scene (e.g., being arrested) when a storm hits, you must return to finish it. Evading via restart will result in a ban.

V. Loot Boxing / RDM / VDM

Randomly attacking or killing players to take loot with little roleplay reasoning is prohibited. You cannot run up and stab/run someone over just to pocket wipe them.

VI. Metagaming

Utilizing information your character did not learn in-game to shape roleplay is prohibited. Immediate ban.

VII. Powergaming / Fail RP

Gaining an unfair advantage using mechanics that leave others no option but to lose.

  • Talking/calling locations while downed (groaning for pain is allowed).
  • Using mechanical knowledge your character hasn't learned through RP.

VIII. New Life Rule (NLR)

A "new life" begins when you bleed out or are body dumped. Your character forgets events leading to that death. You may not return to an ongoing scenario you died in.

IX. Value of Life (NVL)

Treat your character's life as your own. Not fearing for your life (e.g., ignoring 2 guns pointed at you) is prohibited.

X. Criminal Activities

  • Group Limit: Crimes are limited to a max of 8 people (including lookouts/radio comms).
  • Prison Breaks: Not allowed until the transport has actually begun. Breaks must be well-planned.
  • Public Servants: On-duty Police and EMS cannot be robbed/kidnapped unless there are at least 4 PD and 3 EMS on duty.
  • Emergency Vehicles: Stolen emergency vehicles are prohibited unless for emergency transport.
  • Green Zones: No crimes in Clothing Stores, Barber Shops, or Ocean Medical (except Pharmacy).
EXTRA INFO: Gang clothing/colors make you a target for gang-related assumptions. Choose your look wisely.
